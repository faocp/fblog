Common built-in types include:

- `int` for whole numbers
- `float` for decimals
- `str` for text
- `bool` for true/false
- `list`, `tuple`, `dict`, and `set` for collections

Real-world example: `"3" + "4"` gives text concatenation, while `3 + 4` gives arithmetic.
